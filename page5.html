DaeSung

Kang Dae-sung (born April 26, 1989),[1] better known by his stage names Daesung and D-Lite, is a South Korean singer, actor, and television host. Daesung made his musical debut in 2006 as a member of the South Korean boy band Big Bang. He has released a number of solo singles in South Korea under the group's record label YG Entertainment, including "솜사탕" ("Cotton Candy") and "Baby Don't Cry" which peaked at No. 1 and No. 3 on South Korea's Gaon Music Chart, respectively. Since 2013, Daesung has also released one single and three solo albums in Japan under YGEX. His first album, D'scover, peaked at No. 2 on the Japanese Oricon Albums Daily and Weekly Charts. His second and third albums, D'slove and Delight (Japanese:『でぃらいと』), peaked at #1 on the Oricon Albums Daily Chart. Following the success of his albums, Daesung has completed several concert tours throughout Japan.
